# 🚗 Automobile Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze automobile sales performance, market share distribution, and customer insights.

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is divided into three analytical sections:

 **1. Sales & Revenue Analysis**
- Total Revenue KPI
- Revenue Year-over-Year Growth
- Average Sale Price
- Monthly Revenue Trends by Brand
- Market Share Distribution
- Geographic Revenue Analysis
- Price vs Performance Analysis

**2. Market Share & Segment Analysis**
- Total Companies
- Total Units Sold
- Revenue Contribution by Segment
- Market Share by Company
- Units Sold by Segment

**3️. Customer & Performance Analysis**
- Customer Satisfaction Analysis
- Units Sold by Customer Age Group
- Sales by Customer Gender
- Top Performing Models
- Customer Satisfaction by Company

📈 Key Insights
- Identifies top performing automobile brands
- Shows revenue distribution across market segments
- Analyzes customer demographics influencing vehicle purchases
- Highlights price vs performance positioning in the market

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization
